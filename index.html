Chiken Nuggets

Recipe by Chef Areeba

Ingredients

  • Chicken 500 gm
  • Bread crumbs 1 packet
  • Eggs 3
  • Garlic 1 tbsp
  • Ginger 1 tbsp
  • Mustard paste 1 tbsp
  • Bread 2-3 unit
  • Milk 1/2 cup
  • Black pepper A.P.N
  • White pepper A.P.N
  • Salt A.P.N
  • Crushed red chilli A.P.N
  • Corn flour A.P.N

Method:

Add chicken boneless,egg,salt,bread,milk,mustard paste,black pepper,white pepper,crushed red chilli,zeera,garlic,ginger in chooped and chooped.

make Nuggets from this mixture.

For coating

coat first in corn flour,and dip in eggs then add in bread crumb and fry in medium flame.

remove and drain.

serve with ketchup and also fries,coleslaw.
